Zanzibar the Paradise Island!!!

Zanzibar is an Indian Ocean paradise located just off the coast of Tanzania on Africa's east coast. Because of its abundance of produce, spice, and clove plantations, the Zanzibar archipelago is also known as the "Spice Islands.

Zanzibar is endowed with picturesque islands, enticing seas, vibrant coral reefs, and exotic sea creatures, making it the ideal tropical getaway.
